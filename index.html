Brick Crash!!

Game Rules

  • Objective: Clear all the bricks by bouncing the ball off the paddle to hit the bricks. Each brick hit earns you points.
  • Gameplay:
    • Paddle: Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle horizontally along the bottom of the canvas.
    • Ball: The ball bounces off the walls, the paddle, and the bricks. If the ball hits the bottom wall, you lose a life.
  • Bricks:
    • Bricks are initially visible and change colors periodically.
    • When the ball hits a brick, the brick disappears, and you earn points.
  • Scoring: Each brick hit increases your score by 1 point. Clearing all bricks will cause the game to restart with all bricks reappearing.
  • Lives:
    • You start with 3 lives.
    • Losing a life resets the ball and paddle to their starting positions.
    • The game ends when you lose all lives.
  • Game Over: When all lives are lost, the game ends. A "Game Over" message will appear, and you can choose to restart the game.
  • Winning: The game does not have a specific winning condition beyond clearing the bricks and restarting the level.
  • Controls:
    • Move Paddle: Use the left and right arrow keys.
  • Restarting: After the game ends, you can restart or exit the game.
